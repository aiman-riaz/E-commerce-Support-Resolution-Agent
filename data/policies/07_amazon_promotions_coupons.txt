AMAZON.IN PROMOTIONS, COUPONS, AND DEALS POLICY

SECTION 1: AMAZON COUPONS

Amazon coupons help you save on purchases. You can collect coupons from the Coupons Home Page, product detail page, search results, and product listing pages. Coupons provide an instant discount at the time of checkout.

Rewards and Coupons are subject to change. Refer to the individual Terms and Conditions for each coupon for more information including eligibility, minimum order value, and expiry.

SECTION 2: AMAZON PAY CASHBACK

Amazon Pay Cashback only applies to prepaid orders. Amazon Pay cashback offers are mentioned in the Special Offers widget on the product details page. You need to meet the eligibility criteria as described in the offer message and as per the terms and conditions of the offer to avail the cashback.

The cashback is credited to your Amazon Pay balance via an Amazon Gift Card issued by QwikCilver Solutions Private Limited. Visit Amazon Pay Cashback Offer FAQs and Amazon Pay Gift Cards Help pages to know more about cashback and gift cards.

SECTION 3: EXCHANGE OFFERS

Third party buyback partners provide the exchange offer on Amazon and the proposed exchange value. The exchange offer may vary depending on the brand and model selected, your delivery location, and availability of the offer by the buyback partner at the time of placing the order. Refer to the Exchange Offer Frequently Asked Questions Help page to learn how to avail exchange offers.

SECTION 4: LIVE SALE EVENTS

For information on a current sale, refer to the About Amazon Live Sale Events Help page on live deals, promotions, cashback and more. Flash sale prices are only valid during the stated sale window. Amazon does not honour flash sale prices after the sale window ends, including for items left in your cart during the sale.

SECTION 5: COUPON TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Each coupon has its own terms and conditions including:
- Minimum purchase amount
- Eligible product categories
- Expiry date and time
- Whether the coupon is single-use or multi-use
- Whether it can be combined with other offers

Coupons cannot generally be applied to marketplace seller listings unless the seller has specifically opted into the promotion. Coupons may be restricted to specific product categories, brands, or items.

SECTION 6: PROMOTIONS AND RETURNS

If an item purchased with a coupon or promotional discount is returned, the refund is issued for the amount actually paid (after discount). The coupon or promotional discount itself is not refunded as a separate coupon code. Conditions for return still apply as per Amazon's standard returns policy.

SECTION 7: PRICING ERRORS

Occasionally pricing errors may occur due to technical issues. If an item is listed at an incorrectly low price and the error is identified before the order ships, Amazon reserves the right to cancel the order and issue a full refund. Amazon will notify the customer of the cancellation. If the item has already shipped before the error is identified, Amazon will honour the original price.

SECTION 8: PROMOTIONAL ABUSE

Amazon monitors for promotional abuse. Customers found creating multiple accounts to redeem single-use promotions, using automated systems to place promotional orders, or returning items purchased with promotions to exploit credits may have their promotions revoked, orders cancelled, or accounts suspended.

SECTION 9: AMAZON PRIME BENEFITS

Amazon Prime members receive exclusive benefits including free fast delivery on eligible items, access to Prime Video, Prime Music, and exclusive Prime member deals. Prime membership is available at a monthly or annual fee. Benefits and pricing are subject to change. Visit the Amazon Prime Help page for current pricing and benefit details.
