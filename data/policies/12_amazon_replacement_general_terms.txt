AMAZON.IN REPLACEMENT POLICY AND GENERAL TERMS

SECTION 1: REPLACEMENT ELIGIBILITY

Items are eligible for free replacement if:
- The item arrived physically damaged
- The item is defective and does not work as described
- The item is different from what was ordered (wrong item)
- The item has missing parts or accessories
- The item is materially different from the product listing

A free replacement is subject to the same product being available with the same seller. If the seller does not have the exact same product in stock, a refund will be issued instead of a replacement.

SECTION 2: REPLACEMENT PROCESS

To request a replacement:
1. Go to Your Orders
2. Select the relevant order
3. Select Return or Replace items
4. Select the reason for replacement
5. Follow the on-screen instructions

Replacements cannot be delivered to a different address from the original order. If you need the replacement sent to a different address, Amazon recommends returning the item for a refund and placing a new order with the correct address.

SECTION 3: GIFT RETURNS AND REPLACEMENTS

For items eligible for refund or replacement, you can also return a gift item in exchange for an Amazon Pay Gift Card if the gift sender has sent a Gift Receipt to you. For guidance on returning a gift item using a gift receipt, visit Gift Receipts. Gift cards once purchased cannot be cancelled or returned due to regulatory restrictions.

SECTION 4: RETURN SHIPPING FOR REPLACEMENTS

Return shipping costs for replacements are covered by Amazon when the return is due to Amazon's error (wrong item sent, damaged in transit). Replacement postage costs are only reimbursed if the replacement is due to Amazon error. If a prepaid label is not convenient in your location, you can indicate the cost of shipping paid with your replacement item.

SECTION 5: AMAZON DEVICE WARRANTY AND REPLACEMENT

Amazon Devices are covered by a One-Year Limited Warranty. If you are a consumer, you also have consumer rights and this warranty is provided in addition to those rights. If your Amazon Device is faulty, you may be entitled to a repair or replacement from the retailer.

To replace an Amazon Device within the Replacement Period:
1. Erase all personal information from your Amazon Device
2. Place it in the original packaging
3. Visit the Returns Centre on Amazon.in or contact Customer Support
4. Your Amazon Device must be sent using a traceable method

SECTION 6: EXCHANGE OFFERS

Amazon offers exchange deals on select products where you can trade in your old product for a discount on a new one. Exchange offers are provided by third-party buyback partners. The exchange value may vary depending on the brand, model, delivery location, and availability of the offer by the buyback partner at the time of placing the order.

SECTION 7: GENERAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Most items purchased from sellers listed on Amazon are returnable within the return window, except those explicitly identified as not returnable.

Amazon reserves the right to:
- Warn and/or suspend and/or block and/or terminate customer accounts found misusing return or cancellation policies
- Cancel any order before fulfillment due to stock issues, pricing errors, payment failures, or suspected fraud
- Request identity verification before issuing refunds

If Amazon cancels an order after payment has been collected, a full refund will be issued to the original payment method within the applicable refund timeline.

SECTION 8: CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES

Customers are responsible for:
- Providing accurate shipping and billing information at checkout
- Ensuring the shipping address is accessible for delivery
- Reviewing the product listing, return policy, and specifications before purchase
- Reporting issues with orders within the timeframes specified in the relevant policies

Amazon is not responsible for delivery failures, incorrect orders, or other issues arising from inaccurate information provided by the customer.
